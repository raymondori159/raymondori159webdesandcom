About us.
Who are we?
What is our goal?

We are a small group of friends who decided to pursue their dream of opening and managing a hotel that focuses on quality and creating unique experiences for our customers.
We are located in Eastern Europe, more exactly in the neighbourhood called "Carpa»õi" (Carphatians) in the small city of Satu Mare, Romania.
The name is a reference to the three mountain ranges of the Carphatians (Orientali, Meridionali and Occidentali).
Our goal is to provide the best services we can, and show the world that tourism in Easter European is far from boring.

The Rooms

You can choose between two types of rooms:

The family suite: perfect for a romantic couple enjoying a trip. The room is quite big, with modern furniture, a king sized bed, a comfortable couch and a small balcony.

The one bedroom executive suite: perfect for business people who want to rest after a hard day of work. The room is quite small, with modern furniture, single sized bed, and a desk for work purposes.

Prices vary according to seasons. Don't be afraid to contact us for information!
